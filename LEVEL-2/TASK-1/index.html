Tribute to KK

About KK

Krishnakumar Kunnath, popularly known as KK, is a renowned playback singer in the Indian music industry. He was born on August 23, 1968, in Delhi, India. KK is known for his melodious voice and has lent his voice to numerous hit songs in Bollywood.

Notable Achievements

  • Winner of several Filmfare Awards for Best Male Playback Singer
  • Known for hit songs like "Tum Jo Aaye," "Zara Si," and "Pal."
  • Has a huge fan following in India and abroad.

Discography

  • "Tadap Tadap" from "Hum Dil De Chuke Sanam"
  • "Awarapan Banjarapan" from "Jism"
  • "Koi Kahe Kehta Rahe" from "Dil Chahta Hai"

Personal Life

Aside from his successful career in the music industry, KK enjoys a quiet personal life with his family. He is known for his humility and dedication to his art.

Legacy

KK's soulful voice continues to inspire aspiring singers and leaves a lasting legacy in the world of Indian music. His songs have a timeless quality that resonates with music lovers of all generations.
